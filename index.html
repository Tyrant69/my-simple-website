God Of War

Kratos is a fictional character and the protagonist of the popular video game series "God of War," developed by Santa Monica Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. The character was created by David Jaffe and first appeared in the game "God of War," released in 2005 for the PlayStation .

The "God of War" series is known not only for its intense action and combat sequences but also for its compelling narrative and character development. In the 2018 installment of the series, also titled "God of War," Kratos embarks on a journey with his son, Atreus, in the realm of Norse mythology. This game marks a significant departure from the previous installments, introducing a more mature and reflective Kratos, grappling with the consequences of his past actions.
